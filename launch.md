# Temporis Launch Requirements
## Minimum non-engineering requirements for public launch

### Scope
- **Company site:** `temporis.co`
- **Product site:** `vista.temporis.co`

Context:
- Temporis is a US company.
- The company is registered in Delaware and operating in California.
- Vista is a subscription product.
- Vista displays market data to users.
- Vista uses Tiingo data, and launch assumes the correct redistribution/display license is in place.

---

## 1. Shared requirements
These apply across both sites.

### 1. Privacy Policy
A public privacy policy is required.

It should be clearly linked and cover:
- what information is collected,
- why it is collected,
- how it is used,
- whether it is shared with vendors/service providers,
- and how users contact the company about privacy matters.

### 2. Public contact paths
The company should publish clear contact paths for:
- general inquiries,
- support,
- billing,
- and privacy requests.

### 3. Public claims and examples review
Public copy, charts, examples, and product descriptions should be restrained and supportable.

Do not use:
- guaranteed-outcome language,
- exaggerated product claims,
- or casual performance claims.

Any public-facing forecasting examples should be reviewed carefully before launch.

---

## 2. Requirements for `temporis.co`

### 4. Website Terms of Use
`temporis.co` should have website terms covering:
- permitted use,
- intellectual property,
- website disclaimers,
- and limitation of liability.

---

## 3. Requirements for `vista.temporis.co`

### 5. Vista subscription compliance
Vista should have product-specific subscription terms and customer-facing subscription disclosures.

This should cover:
- subscription structure,
- billing,
- renewals,
- cancellations,
- refunds or no-refund policy,
- account rules,
- and service limitations.

Before launch, the Vista purchase flow should also clearly support:
- clear renewal/disclosure terms before sign-up,
- affirmative user consent,
- post-purchase acknowledgment,
- and a clear cancellation path.

### 6. Forecasting Disclaimer / Risk Disclosure
Vista should clearly state that:
- forecasts are informational,
- forecasts are not guaranteed,
- users remain responsible for their own decisions,
- and market outcomes are uncertain.

### 7. Market data licensing readiness
Before launch, confirm that the Tiingo license used is the correct one for user-facing display/redistribution in Vista.

Also confirm any required:
- attribution,
- notice,
- display restrictions,
- caching restrictions,
- or redistribution limits.

---

## 4. Launch order
Recommended order:
1. Confirm Tiingo licensing/data-rights readiness.
2. Finalize Privacy Policy.
3. Finalize Vista subscription compliance items.
4. Finalize Forecasting Disclaimer / Risk Disclosure.
5. Finalize Website Terms of Use for `temporis.co`.
6. Publish clear contact paths on both sites.
7. Final review of public copy, charts, and examples.
