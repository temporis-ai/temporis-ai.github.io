Temporis Arc Pair Refinements

These refinements push `Direction J` toward a more elegant, brand-like symbol. The focus is on cleaner arc proportion, heavier geometry for small-size performance, and a stronger silhouette without losing the sense of temporal continuity.

Refinement 1

Broader Arc

The lower arc becomes broader and more grounded. This improves stability and makes the overall mark feel less delicate.

Most stable and most usable of the set. It preserves the elegance of the arc idea while strengthening the base shape.

Best balance of elegance and practical readability.

Refinement 2

Closer Pair

The two arcs sit closer together, making the composition feel more like one symbol and less like two independent parts.

This is the most cohesive composition. It feels more intentionally constructed, though also slightly less airy than the original.

Most “logo-like” composition, but slightly less open.

Refinement 3

Heavier Geometry

The same arc relationship, but with visibly heavier thickness and shorter spans. This is the most practical version for tiny sizes.

The most robust technical refinement. It sacrifices some elegance, but it should hold up better in app and favicon use.

Best if practical robustness matters more than delicacy.
