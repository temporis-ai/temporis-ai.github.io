Concept 02 / Temporal Grid v4

Temporal Grid v4

Evaluation: the hierarchy needed to be clearer. This version makes the `T` visibly larger and lets the lower `s` logic become shorter and subordinate underneath it. That gives the mark a better structural story and makes it easier to read quickly.

TEMPORIS

Why It Works

  • The `T` is now clearly dominant, which improves recognition and hierarchy.
  • The shorter lower form reads as a secondary layer under the `T`, not as an equal competing shape.
  • The symbol still feels computational and AI-adjacent without cliché robotics or chatbot imagery.
  • The lockup is easier to parse quickly, especially at smaller sizes.

Risk

  • The `TS` story is still implicit, which is acceptable if the rest of the brand system reinforces it.
  • The wordmark can still be custom-drawn further if you want a final polished identity rather than a strong concept study.
