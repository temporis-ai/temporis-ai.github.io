Concept 04 / Signal Slash TS

Signal Slash TS

This direction follows the structure in `Gemini.png` much more directly: a clear `TS` monogram, with a single time-series stroke slicing through it. The goal here is not hidden abstraction. It is a direct, memorable monogram with one strong motion cue.

TEMPORIS

Why It Works

  • The `TS` reading is immediate.
  • The wave stroke ties the monogram directly to time series without extra symbols.
  • The silhouette is bold and memorable.
  • This is the closest translation of the `Gemini.png` reference.

Risk

  • This is less restrained than the abstract directions.
  • If over-refined, it could drift toward software-company logo cliché.

One-Color Mark

The concept still holds in one color, which is important.

Dark Surface

The slash line keeps the mark alive on dark surfaces.

App Icon Crop

Best if you want a direct, branded monogram instead of a quieter abstract symbol.
