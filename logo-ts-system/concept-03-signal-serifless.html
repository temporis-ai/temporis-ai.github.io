Concept 03 / Orbit Weave

Orbit Weave

This is the most abstract and the closest to the “looks like AI” brief. The mark behaves more like an orbital intelligence symbol than a monogram, but it still keeps a quiet vertical `T` backbone and a time-series-like looping path.

TEMPORIS

Why It Works

  • This is the strongest match to the idea of an AI-looking company symbol.
  • The loop structure feels intelligent and dynamic without becoming a literal chatbot or spark icon.
  • The terminal node gives it a memorable endpoint and a more ownable silhouette.
  • The wordmark stays restrained enough to keep the whole system serious.

Risk

  • This is the easiest one to over-style.
  • The looping move has to stay thick and simple or it will become decorative.
