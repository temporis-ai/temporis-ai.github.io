Product

Forecasting turned into products people can use.

Vista and Platform are two products built for different users and workflows.

Temporis is starting with financial markets.

Vista

A forecast gallery that shows forecasts in charts.

Vista is built for short-term traders. It helps users read forecasts, explore them visually, and bring predictions into strategy.

  • Forecast gallery format.
  • Built for short-term traders.
  • Designed to make predictions easier to use in strategy.
Platform

An API product for developers.

Platform provides direct access to Collie. It lets teams control model behavior and integrate forecasting into their own systems.

  • Access Collie directly.
  • Control model behavior with parameters.
  • Integrate forecasting into existing systems.
