Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 9, 2026

Scope

This policy describes how Temporis handles information collected through the Temporis website and product surfaces that link to this policy, including Vista.

Information we collect

Temporis may collect contact information you provide, account identity information returned by shared sign-in services, subscription and billing-state information needed to provide paid access, and technical data such as IP address, browser details, request metadata, and service logs.

How we use information

Temporis uses this information to operate the website and products, authenticate users, enforce product access, support billing flows, respond to support or privacy requests, improve reliability, and protect the services from misuse.

Service providers

Temporis may use service providers for hosting, analytics, authentication, billing, security, and support operations. Payment card collection is handled through Stripe-hosted flows where applicable; Temporis products do not directly collect payment card details when those hosted flows are used.

Contact

Privacy questions, requests, and general inquiries can be sent to hello@temporis.co.
