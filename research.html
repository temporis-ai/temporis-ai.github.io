Research

Introducing Collie, our time series forecasting foundation model.

Collie is the core forecasting model at Temporis. It is built to advance the state of the art in forecasting.

1

Time series

A time series is a sequence of numbers that changes over time, such as prices, demand, or weather.

2

Multivariate

Multivariate means Collie can work with many related time series at once, rather than looking at only one in isolation.

3

Multimodal

Multimodal means Collie can combine different kinds of data, such as prices and text like news, when producing forecasts.

Why It Matters

Research that matters over time.

These capabilities make Collie useful across a wider range of forecasting tasks, and the added context can lead to stronger forecasts.

The goal is to build forecasting technology that can grow stronger over time as it works with richer data and broader use cases.
